What exactly is Examforge? add
Examforge is a high-performance educational platform designed to help Nigerian students master JAMB, WAEC, and specialized University courses through rigorous testing and analytics.
Does it cover 200-level University courses? add
Yes. We have specialized modules for courses, specifically tailored for university curricula.
Can I use the platform without internet? add
As a Progressive Web App (PWA), Examforge supports offline caching, allowing you to access previously loaded study materials and quizzes without an active data connection.
How realistic is the CBT environment? add
The interface is modeled directly after standard CBT software used in JAMB and professional examinations to build your confidence and timing.
Are the past questions verified? add
Our database is sourced from official past questions and verified by subject matter experts to ensure accuracy in both the questions and the explanations provided.
Is there a mobile app? add
You can install Examforge directly to your home screen using the "Add to Home Screen" prompt on your browser, giving you a full-screen, app-like experience.
How do I track my performance? add
After every session, the system provides a detailed breakdown of your score, identifying weak topics that require more discipline.
Is my study progress saved? add
Yes, as long as you are logged into your account, your quiz history and analytics are synchronized across all your devices.
How can I suggest a new course? add
We are constantly expanding. You can suggest a specific course outline or subject via the contact form at the bottom of this page.
Who is behind Examforge? add
Examforge is developed in Awka, Anambra State, by a team dedicated to engineering better educational outcomes for African students.
