DATA_INTEGRITY_LOG: 2026.05.10 // LOCATION: AWKA_NG

01 // DATA TRANSMISSION

When you interact with the Examforge network, we ingest specific data points to optimize your study experience. This includes:

  • Identification data (Name and Email address).
  • Academic preferences (Courses like MAT 102 or MCB 222).
  • Performance metrics (CBT scores and timing analytics).

02 // SYSTEM UTILIZATION

Your data is used solely for the refinement of your educational path. The Forge utilizes this information to:

  • Generate accurate performance charts for subject areas like Microbiology.
  • Sync your progress across devices via the cloud.
  • Verify your identity during high-stakes CBT simulations.

03 // LOCAL STORAGE & PWA

As a Progressive Web App, Examforge utilizes local storage and service workers to cache assets for offline use. This allows students in areas with intermittent connectivity, such as parts of Anambra State, to maintain their study momentum without a live connection.

04 // THIRD-PARTY ARCHIVES

We leverage industrial-grade infrastructure to secure your data:

  • Firebase: For secure authentication and database hosting.
  • EmailJS: For direct communication between the student and the Forge support desk.

05 // ARCHIVE RETENTION

Your academic records remain in the Forge matrix as long as your account is active. You maintain the right to request a complete "Data Purge"—permanently deleting your account and all associated performance history from our archives.

06 // COMPLIANCE

This protocol is governed by the data protection standards of the Federal Republic of Nigeria. All system operations are conducted with transparency and discipline.
